Frequently Asked Questions

What is CouchSurfing?

CouchSurfing is a platform that connects travelers with hosts willing to offer them a place to stay for free.

Is CouchSurfing safe?

Yes! We encourage users to verify their profiles and read reviews before hosting or staying with someone.

How do I find a host?

You can search for hosts by location and send them a request to stay.

Does CouchSurfing charge a fee?

No, CouchSurfing itself is free. However, you can tip the host if you want.

Can I be both a host and a traveler?

Absolutely! You can offer your space when available and travel when you want.
